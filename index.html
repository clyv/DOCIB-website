About Us

We are a healthcare conglomerate with a single goal: to encourage people to live healthier lives.

DOCIB is a market leader in the rapidly expanding UAE healthcare business. Across the Emirates, we provide by far the most extensive variety of services, including Pharmacies, Clinics, and Nutrition Centers.

Lulu Pharmacies
Everything you require to maintain your health and happiness. Our pharmacy team is highly educated to provide accurate and appropriate information. We have your back!
